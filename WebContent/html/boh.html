Facilities

Dance
Children are trained in Folk, Light Classical, and Classical dances during their dance periods and as a part of the work experience classes. The teachers ensure that the children take part in various competitions in and out of school.

Clinic
Keeping in mind the health and well being of every individual in the school premises there is a well equipped clinic with 3 bed capacity. We have a well trained nurse who tenders the mothers touch to the children.

Music
In our school we teach vocal music from I st to X th Std student. They learn selected song for every festival and occasion. No only prayer song and patriotic & folk song, we also teach our children pure classical, semi classical, selected Bhajan, caroll, Alankar Basic knowledge of classical music, Khyaal & Bandish. Beside this we also give knowledge of Jal. We encourage them to perform in every occasion to be a good performer.

Achievements
For the past few years our student have been participating in various solo song, Grouping, light classical, classical, Intra-school and Inter school and others competitions. Winning awards for the school at all prizes at inter-school level organized by Samannarg, Nrityanjali and Rotart Clubs etc.
